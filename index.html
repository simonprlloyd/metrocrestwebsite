Frequently Asked Questions

Answers for Brides,
Businesses & Everyone Else.

The questions we get most often — about wedding dress preservation pricing, commercial laundry accounts, rug cleaning timelines, and more.

How much does wedding dress cleaning and preservation cost in Addison, TX?+
Wedding dress cleaning and preservation at Metrocrest typically ranges from $250 to $600 depending on the gown's fabric, embellishments, beadwork, and condition. Every gown is hand-inspected before pricing — we offer free consultations and quotes by appointment. Heavily beaded or hand-embroidered gowns and gowns with stains, mud, or wine require additional treatment, which is reflected in the final estimate.
Do you offer wedding dress preservation near Dallas and Plano?+
Yes. Metrocrest is located in Addison, just minutes from Dallas, Plano, Frisco, Richardson, Carrollton, Farmers Branch, and the wider DFW Metroplex. We use museum-grade, acid-free preservation boxing with a viewing window so your gown is heirloom-ready for decades. Brides regularly drive in from across North Texas because we handle the full lifecycle — cleaning, preservation, and any later repairs — under one roof.
Do you have a drive-thru for dry cleaning?+
Yes — Metrocrest offers a convenient drive-thru at our Addison location for dropping off and picking up dry cleaning without leaving your car. The drive-thru is available during all standard business hours. It's one of the only drive-thru dry cleaners in the Addison and North Dallas area.
What commercial laundry services do you offer for Dallas-area businesses?+
We provide commercial dry cleaning and wash-and-fold services for hotels, restaurants, salons, medical practices, gyms, and corporate offices throughout the DFW Metroplex. Services include linens, uniforms, chef whites, scrubs, robes, towels, banquet linens, and napery. Accounts include scheduled pickup and delivery, contract pricing, garment-level barcoding, and a dedicated account manager who actually answers the phone. Standard turnaround is 48 hours.
Do you clean Persian and Oriental rugs?+
Yes. Metrocrest specializes in hand-washed cleaning for fine rugs including Persian, Oriental, hand-knotted, antique, and modern pieces. Our in-plant process includes thorough dusting, hand-washing with rug-safe shampoo, flat-rack drying, fringe repair, and pet-odor treatment. We offer free pickup and delivery across Addison, Dallas, Plano, and the surrounding Metroplex. Most rugs are returned within 7–10 business days.
Can you clean leather jackets, suede, and fur coats?+
Yes. Our leather atelier handles specialty cleaning and restoration for leather jackets, suede garments, shearling coats, and fur. Services include color refresh, stain treatment, conditioning, reshaping, and minor repair, performed by technicians trained in heritage leatherwork. Pricing varies by garment — we provide free quotes at drop-off.
Do you host weddings and events at the Metrocrest event center?+
Yes. Our event center hosts weddings, rehearsal dinners, anniversaries, milestone birthdays, bridal showers, and corporate events. Our in-house catering kitchen provides plated dinners, buffets, cocktail receptions, and licensed bar service. The venue pairs naturally with our Bridal House and on-site apartment suites for full-weekend wedding experiences in Addison.
Do you offer extended-stay apartments or hotel rooms in Addison?+
Yes. We offer furnished apartment suites and boutique hotel-style rooms in Addison, TX, available by the night, week, or month. They are popular with visiting family, wedding parties, traveling professionals, and extended business stays. All linens and laundry are handled by our in-house commercial laundry — so everything is hospital-clean and hotel-fresh.
What are Metrocrest's hours in Addison, TX?+
Metrocrest is open Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM; Wednesday and Friday from 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM; and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Closed Sundays. Bridal salon, event center, and hospitality services are available by appointment outside of standard hours.
Where is Metrocrest located?+
Metrocrest is located at 3709 Spring Valley Rd, Addison, TX 75001 — minutes from Dallas, Plano, Carrollton, Richardson, Farmers Branch, and Frisco. Call (972) 243-1982 for directions or to schedule any service.
Do you offer same-day dry cleaning service?+
Yes. Drop off most dry cleaning items before 9:00 AM on weekdays and they'll be ready the same day. Items requiring specialty work — wedding gowns, leather, suede, rugs, heavily soiled garments — require standard turnaround.
