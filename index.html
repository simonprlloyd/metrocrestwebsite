Frequently Asked

Answers for brides,
businesses & everyone else.

Where is the best wedding dress cleaners in Addison, TX?+
Metrocrest in Addison has cleaned and preserved wedding gowns for brides across the DFW Metroplex since 1999. Every gown is hand-inspected by our bridal specialists, cleaned according to its fabric and embellishments, and preserved in acid-free, museum-grade boxing. Located at 3709 Spring Valley Rd, Addison, TX 75001.
How much does wedding dress cleaning and preservation cost?+
Wedding dress cleaning and preservation at Metrocrest typically ranges from $250 to $600 depending on the gown's fabric, embellishments, beadwork, and condition. Every gown is hand-inspected before pricing — we offer free consultations by appointment. Call (972) 243-1982 to book.
Do you do bridal alterations and tailoring?+
Yes. Our in-house bridal alterations team handles wedding gown fittings, bustles, hems, bodice and strap adjustments, and full custom tailoring. We also alter bridesmaid dresses, mother-of-the-bride gowns, and groom's suits — full bridal party coordination available. By appointment.
Do you clean wedding linens, tablecloths, and event fabric?+
Yes. We clean tablecloths, napkins, runners, chair covers, ceremony fabrics, and event linens for weddings, rehearsal dinners, and corporate events. Pickup and delivery available across Addison, Dallas, Plano, and the DFW Metroplex. Volume pricing for event planners and venues.
Do you offer same-day dry cleaning in Addison?+
Yes. Drop off most dry cleaning items before 9:00 AM Monday through Friday and they'll be ready the same day at no extra charge. Same-day service applies to standard dry cleaning — wedding gowns, leather, rugs, and heavily soiled garments require standard turnaround.
Do you have a drive-thru dry cleaners in Addison?+
Yes. Metrocrest offers a drive-thru at our Addison location for dropping off and picking up dry cleaning without leaving your car — one of the only drive-thru dry cleaners in Addison and North Dallas.
How much does dry cleaning cost in Addison, TX?+
Typical pricing at Metrocrest: dress shirts from $3, pants/slacks from $7, suits from $14, dresses from $12, and coats from $16. Specialty garments, wedding gowns, leather, and rugs are priced individually. Call (972) 243-1982 for a specific quote.
Do you offer wash and fold laundry service?+
Yes. Metrocrest offers professional wash & fold laundry for households and businesses across Addison, Dallas, Plano, Carrollton, and the DFW Metroplex. We wash, dry, and neatly fold your laundry — typically returned within 24 to 48 hours.
Do you clean Persian and Oriental rugs?+
Yes. We specialize in hand-washed cleaning for fine rugs including Persian, Oriental, hand-knotted, antique, and modern pieces. Pickup and delivery across Addison and the greater Dallas area.
Can you clean leather jackets, suede, and fur?+
Yes. Our specialists handle cleaning and restoration for leather jackets, suede garments, shearling coats, and fur. Services include color refresh, stain treatment, conditioning, and reshaping.
What are Metrocrest's hours in Addison, TX?+
Monday through Friday: 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Saturday: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Closed Sundays. Bridal consultations available by appointment outside standard hours.
Where is Metrocrest located?+
Metrocrest is at 3709 Spring Valley Rd, Addison, TX 75001 — minutes from Dallas, Plano, Carrollton, Richardson, and Farmers Branch. Call (972) 243-1982.
